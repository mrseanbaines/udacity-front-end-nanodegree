French Bulldog

French Bulldogs are sweet-natured, affectionate dogs that enjoy playing and cuddling in equal measures!

  • Scientific name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Life span: 10 – 12 years
  • Origin: France, England
  • Hypoallergenic: No

French Bulldogs, or ‘Frenchies’ as they’re more affectionately known, are loving family dogs and will tolerate well-behaved children, given the correct socialisation as a puppy.
